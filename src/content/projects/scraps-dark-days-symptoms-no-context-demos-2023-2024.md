This page holds a later Scraps EP built from rap demos recorded across 2023 and 2024. The center of gravity is still the songs: dark-day demos, symptoms pieces, no-context cuts, and unfinished recordings that belong together because they came from the same pressure.

The archival framing matters, but it serves the music. This release extends the line opened by The Death Of My Feminine Demos (2022) and SCRAPS AFTER HEARTBREAKS MUSICVIDEO, keeping the contradictions audible instead of sanding them down.
