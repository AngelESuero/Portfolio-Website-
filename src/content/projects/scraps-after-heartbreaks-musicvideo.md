This page holds the music-video release for one of the rap demos from the Scraps EP run, where the heartbreak and isolation writing starts taking on a clearer visual form.

The video does not replace the demos around it. It sharpens one of them and lets the release look outward. It sits between The Death Of My Feminine Demos (2022) and the larger 2023–2024 Scraps EP as the point where the material becomes more visibly public without losing its rawness.
